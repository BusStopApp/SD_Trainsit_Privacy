# SD Trainsit Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **San Diego Transit** ("the Application," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and respected.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information Collected Automatically
When you use the Application, we may automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:
*   **Device Information**: Operating system version, device model, and unique device identifiers (such as IDFA for iOS or GAID for Android).
*   **Log Information**: Details of how you use our service, such as the bus routes or stops you search for, and the time/frequency of access.
*   **Network Information**: IP address, network type (e.g., Wi-Fi, 4G/5G).

### 1.2 Information You Proactively Provide
The Application **does not require** user registration or account creation. Your "Favorites" and personalized settings are **stored locally on your device** (using industry-standard local storage technology). We do not store your personal identity information on our servers.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:
*   **To Provide Services**: To deliver real-time transit arrival information and stop locations.
*   **To Improve the App**: To analyze app performance, fix bugs, and optimize the user experience.
*   **To Display Advertisements**: To show relevant advertisements through our third-party advertising partners.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers

The Application includes integrated services from third-party partners who may collect information used to identify your device:

*   **Google AdMob**: Used for advertising support. Google may use your device's advertising identifier to show personalized ads.
*   **Transit Data Providers**: We use third-party APIs (such as `mytransitlive.com`) to retrieve real-time transit data. Necessary network metadata may be transmitted during these data requests.

You can review the privacy policies of these partners here:
*   [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Data Storage and Security

*   **Local Storage**: Your favorite stops and history are saved only on your phone. If you uninstall the Application, this locally stored data will be deleted.
*   **Transmission Security**: We use encrypted protocols (HTTPS) for communication between the Application and backend servers to ensure data security during transmission.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Application. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
